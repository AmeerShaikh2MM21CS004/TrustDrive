How Trust Drive Works

Trust Drive is a blockchain-powered ride-sharing platform that ensures secure, transparent, and decentralized transactions using Ethereum cryptocurrency.

All transactions on Trust Drive are processed using smart contracts on the Ethereum blockchain. This ensures:

  • Transparency: Every ride and payment is recorded on the blockchain, making it tamper-proof.
  • Security: No third-party interference, ensuring secure payments.
  • Instant Transactions: Payments are made directly between drivers and passengers using Ethereum.
